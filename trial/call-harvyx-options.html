How to pick

  • 3A — Inline CTA: Most editorial / luxury. Lives next to the headline, scrolls naturally. Zero overlap, zero floating widget anywhere. Best if you want maximum cleanliness and you're OK with the call action only existing inside the hero + footer.
  • 3B — Quiet corner anchor: Small badge pinned to the top-right inside the hero card only. Out of the way of the headline, brand-coloured, never crosses into another section. Disappears after you scroll past hero. Good if you want a persistent-feeling phone link that doesn't dominate.
  • 3C — Smart cut-zone floating: The "old" floating widget but with rules — it auto-hides on dark sections (Hero, Operating Model, Trade Map) and only fades in on the light sections (Industry Grid, Wheel, Contact). User always has a click target without ever overlapping a luxury panel.
My recommendation: 3B. It's anchored to the hero like 3A but doesn't compete with the headline composition. Premium-brand sites (Bottega, Hermès, McKinsey) all use this kind of quiet top-right link rather than a floating bubble. If you want a persistent call action across the whole page, then 3C. Don't pick 3A if you want users to be able to call mid-page.
