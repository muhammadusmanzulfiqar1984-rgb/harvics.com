Design Principles

Geometric Foundation: The H mark uses clean rectangles, inspired by Apple's precision and Square's boldness.

Premium Minimalism: No gradients, no 3D effects, no noise. Pure form follows function.

Color Strategy: Maroon conveys trust and heritage. Gold accent adds premium positioning without ostentation.

Versatility: Works at any size from 16px favicon to billboard. Maintains clarity in single color or full color.

Scalability: SVG format ensures perfect rendering on any device, from retina displays to 4K monitors.
