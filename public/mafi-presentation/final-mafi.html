Weekly Dashboard
Every Monday morning: pipeline value, conversion probability, next actions, blockers. One contact, full accountability.

Section 09

Our Approach

Eight principles that define how Harvics operates — distinguishing this engagement from any conventional agent, broker, or trading-house arrangement.

01
Selective, Not General
Each supply-side partner assessed selectively, structured selectively, activated only within agreed scope. No open-book engagement.
02
Product-Specific, Not Broad
Each MAFI product line treated separately. No category pushed through the same route or buyer class as another.
03
Market-Routed, Not Random
Each category assigned to the market, buyer type, and commercial path most likely to produce qualified revenue — before activation.
04
Controlled Activation, Not Open Circulation
Buyer access opened in a controlled sequence. Market access never released casually or without structure.
05
Dedicated Handling, Not Shared Attention
A supplier of MAFI's scale is not handled as one of many undifferentiated accounts. Dedicated capacity ensures continuity and accountability.
06
Commercial Before Transactional
First priority: architect the route properly. Transaction comes after route discipline, not before it.
07
Retained Discipline, Not Speculative
Market architecture, buyer activation, and execution capacity deployed only under formal engagement — never on open-ended terms.
08
Scale Only on Performance
Territorial rights, broader activation, and exclusivity assessed and expanded only where performance and commercial discipline justify them.
