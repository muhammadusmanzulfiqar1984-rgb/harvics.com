Section 01

The Harvics × MAFI Product Routing Map

Each product category is treated as a completely separate commercial vertical. Every line is mapped to a precise market, buyer class, route-to-market model, and price/margin architecture before a single introduction is activated.

Germany leads European tomato paste imports at 18% share — the single largest market. Egypt is not China. European buyers are actively diversifying away from Chinese tomato paste, and Egyptian origin — Mediterranean, clean, LEED Gold certified — currently commands a premium in Germany and the UK.

Germany — 18% share

Italy / Spain — 14% combined

Growing processed-food industry. Lower price point but extremely high volume. Ideal for clearing production that does not meet EU premium specifications.

Section 02

The Harvics Dedicated Desk Model

30% Harvics proprietary commercial infrastructure. This is not a trading-house model. This is a dedicated commercial engine that operates like MAFI's own European sales office — without MAFI having to build it, staff it, or manage it.

01
Full-Time Dedication
One senior commercial desk lead, 100% allocated to MAFI. One supporting analyst/coordinator. Zero shared portfolio responsibility — the desk lead handles no other supplier, category, or client simultaneously.
02
Dedicated Infrastructure
Custom CRM pipeline locked to MAFI. Dedicated email alias, dedicated communication channels, dedicated shared drive with MAFI-specific routing maps, buyer dossiers, and compliance folders.
03
Buyer Database
200+ pre-qualified accounts across Germany, UK, Scandinavia, GCC, USA, and Asia-Pacific — all segmented by category and buyer class. Updated weekly. Proprietary to Harvics.
04
Weekly Dashboard
Every Monday morning: pipeline value, conversion probability, next actions, blockers. MAFI receives a single point of truth — one contact, full accountability, no chasing.
05
Scalable Structure
Phase II: lead + coordinator. Phase III: add transaction coordinator and logistics interface. Phase IV: full mini-team of 3–4 people, still 100% dedicated to MAFI.
06
Performance-Gated
After the 3-month minimum, if qualified discussions have not been opened with at least 70% of the priority target list, MAFI may review and exit without further obligation beyond fees already paid.
When Harvics onboards MAFI, we don't add your products to a shared trading desk. We build you a dedicated commercial sales office inside Harvics. One senior person works on MAFI and only MAFI, full time. They live inside the routing map we created together. They know every buyer, every specification window, every margin logic. You get all the benefit of a professional European sales team without hiring anyone, without paying salaries, without managing infrastructure. This dedicated desk is what the retainer actually buys — not vague promises, but owned commercial capacity. That is the Harvics difference.

Funding: The entire desk is funded exclusively through the Phase II Retained Market Activation retainer (USD 20,000/month, 3-month minimum). This is not a commission or success fee — it is the infrastructure fee that delivers a zero-overhead European export sales team working only on MAFI's portfolio.

Network Protection: All buyer data, routing logic, and commercial architecture remain Harvics proprietary assets. MAFI receives outputs and results — never raw network files unless exclusivity is later granted in writing.

Section 04

Commercial Model

Phased, retained, and performance-linked. Designed to progress from qualification to activation, from activation to execution, and from execution to scale only where commercial discipline and results justify expansion.

Phase I Commercial Admission & Strategic Qualification USD 25,000

One-time admission fee covering the initial onboarding and structuring of the engagement. Payable in advance. Non-refundable upon activation.

  • Mandate intake and category, territory & buyer-class review
  • Product-to-market fit analysis and routing architecture
  • Commercial framework design and activation-readiness review
  • Exclusivity feasibility assessment
  • Output: qualification note, scope matrix, routing framework, engagement-path recommendation
Phase II Retained Market Activation USD 20,000/mo

Monthly retainer covering live commercial activation. Minimum 3-month commitment. Payable monthly in advance. This phase activates the Dedicated Desk.

  • Target account mapping and buyer prioritization
  • Controlled introductions and meeting coordination
  • Commercial follow-up and pipeline development
  • Sample & compliance coordination
  • First-order pathway support
  • Output: active pipeline, qualified buyer discussions, weekly dashboard
Phase III Execution & Transaction Conversion USD 25,000+/mo

Applies once commercial activity matures into executable business. Economics defined by category complexity, geography, transaction size, exclusivity rights, and execution burden.

  • Transaction structuring and order-path support
  • Principal-buyer or distribution route handling
  • Alignment on terms, sequencing, and execution logic
  • Commercial basis: execution retainer / transaction-linked fee / principal-buying spread / distribution economics / hybrid
  • Output: first executable transaction route, revenue conversion framework
Phase IV Scale, Rights & Exclusivity Negotiated

Applies only after performance, alignment, and commercial validity are established. Not automatic. Subject to written confirmation only.

  • Territorial and category expansion
  • Exclusivity discussion and performance thresholds
  • Review rights and continuation or withdrawal rights
  • Revised commercial economics where scale justifies them
  • Output: expansion recommendation, exclusivity assessment, revised rights structure

Section 07

Exclusivity, Confidentiality & Non-Circumvention

No Automatic Exclusivity
No exclusivity, territorial reservation, category protection, buyer protection, or channel protection shall arise automatically by discussion, presentation, meeting, payment of an initial fee, or partial engagement.
Exclusivity by Written Grant Only
Any exclusivity right must be expressly granted in writing by Harvics, limited by category/territory/channel, subject to performance conditions, and subject to review, suspension, or withdrawal rights.
Confidentiality
All materials, structures, routing logic, engagement models, pricing concepts, buyer classifications, territory strategies, and commercial information shared by Harvics are treated as confidential and may not be disclosed or used outside the agreed engagement.
Protection of Buyer Network
Harvics' buyer network, account intelligence, routing framework, and market-access information are proprietary and protected. Buyer names shall not be disclosed on an unrestricted basis. Access opened only within formal engagement.
Non-Circumvention
MAFI shall not directly or indirectly approach buyers introduced by Harvics outside the agreed framework, use Harvics' routing logic to transact independently, or use third parties to replicate or bypass Harvics' role within agreed scope.
Rights in Materials
All commercial models, engagement structures, routing maps, account strategies, and related materials produced by Harvics remain the property of Harvics unless otherwise expressly agreed in writing.

In the event of breach: Harvics reserves the right to suspend all activity immediately, terminate the engagement, withdraw any exclusivity or reservation rights, withhold further access, and pursue all available commercial and legal remedies. Confidentiality and non-circumvention obligations survive termination.

Section 09

Our Approach

Eight principles that define how Harvics operates — and what distinguishes this engagement from any conventional agent, broker, or trading-house arrangement.

01
Selective, Not General
Harvics does not approach this engagement as a general trading or brokerage exercise. Each supply-side partner is assessed selectively, structured selectively, and activated only within agreed scope.
02
Product-Specific, Not Broad
Each MAFI product line is treated separately. Tomato concentrate, citrus concentrates, IQF, freeze-dried products, and purées are not pushed through the same route or buyer class.
03
Market-Routed, Not Random
Harvics follows a disciplined routing model in which each category is assigned to the market, buyer type, and commercial path most likely to produce qualified revenue.
04
Controlled Activation, Not Open Circulation
Buyer access is opened in a controlled sequence. Harvics does not release market access casually or circulate products broadly without structure.
05
Dedicated Handling, Not Shared Attention
A supplier of MAFI's scale is not handled as one of many undifferentiated accounts. Harvics establishes dedicated handling capacity so that continuity, follow-up, and accountability remain intact.
06
Commercial Before Transactional
The first priority is to architect the route properly — category, market, buyer class, and commercial structure. Transaction comes after route discipline, not before it.
07
Retained Discipline, Not Speculative
Harvics works under retained commercial discipline. Market architecture, buyer activation, and execution capacity are deployed only under formal engagement.
08
Scale Only on Performance
Territorial rights, broader activation, and exclusivity are not presumed at the outset. They are assessed and expanded only where performance, alignment, and commercial discipline justify them.
