Production · Capabilities

What we make —
and how

All washing, finishing, and surface treatment is done in-house. Nothing is outsourced. This is what brand compliance teams need to know.

01

Laser Cutting & Distressing

In-house laser systems create geometric patterns, cultural motifs, and worn-in effects without chemical abrasion — preserving fabric integrity while enabling brand-specific design signatures.

In-HouseChemical-FreePrecision ±0.1mm
02

Enzyme & Eco Wash

Bio-based enzyme washes create natural colour gradients and fading effects with 60% less water than conventional stone washing. Each recipe is documented and reproducible across production runs.

Water-SavingBio-BasedReproducible
03

Premium Surface Treatments

Crinkling, abrasion, cracking, fog coating, heat pressing — all applied in-house. The same workshop produces for domestic retail and international export to the same standard.

CrinklingFog CoatingHeat Press
04

Design & ODM Development

In-house design team and R&D engineers handle tech packs, sampling, and seasonal development. Small-batch sampling to large-scale production without change in quality control team.

ODM ReadyFOBCMPT

AQL 1.5 is the quality standard used by luxury and Tier A brands — most factories only offer AQL 2.5. Every garment is inspected for durability, shrinkage, colourfastness, and wearer safety.
