Why offer LDP if net margin is almost identical to FOB?

The per-piece net difference is marginal (€12.38 vs €12.50). The value is entirely strategic: EU buyers who receive LDP/DDP do not switch suppliers. Their logistics teams are integrated with her dispatch schedule. Their finance teams process one invoice with no surprise duty bills. LDP makes the relationship structurally sticky. That is the reason — not per-piece margin gain.
