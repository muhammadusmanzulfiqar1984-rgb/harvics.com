Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Harvics actually do?

Harvics Global Ventures is an AI-powered global sourcing and supply chain execution platform. We connect retailers and brands to verified factory partners across 40+ countries — handling sourcing, procurement, QA, logistics, and compliance from a single operating layer.

What is the minimum order quantity?

MOQs vary by product category. Leather & Knit start from 500 units per style; Denim & Workwear from 1,000 units; Footwear from 300 pairs. Contact us for a tailored brief and we'll identify the right factory tier for your volume.

How long does sourcing take?

Our 48-hour sourcing response model means you receive a qualified supplier shortlist within 2 business days of briefing. Production lead times typically range from 45 to 120 days depending on category, volume, and complexity.

Which certifications do your factories hold?

Our factory network holds ISO 9001, SA 8000, SEDEX SMETA, OEKO-TEX, ZDHC, and bluesign® across various product verticals. We match your compliance requirements to the appropriate certified facility.

Do you handle logistics and freight?

Yes. Harvics provides end-to-end supply chain command including DC management, freight forwarding, hubbing, consolidation, and eLogistics with real-time visibility at every node.

What markets do you operate in?

We operate across 40+ countries with Continental HQs in London, Milan, New York, Karachi, and Hong Kong. Primary manufacturing hubs include Pakistan, Bangladesh, Vietnam, Turkey, China, and India.

How does quality control work?

Quality is embedded at every stage — inline production inspections, pre-shipment audits, lab testing, and final approval sign-off. We have dedicated QA teams in each key manufacturing corridor.

Can I visit the factories?

Absolutely. We coordinate factory visits, virtual facility tours, and supplier audits for qualified partners. Reach out via the form and we'll arrange access through our local office teams.
