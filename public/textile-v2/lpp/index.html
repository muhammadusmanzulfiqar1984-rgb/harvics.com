CSRD Is Not a Reporting Exercise. It Is a Criminal Liability.

Under EU CSRD and the forthcoming Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence Directive, non-compliance is not a footnote risk. Regulators have subpoena power across supply chain tiers. Annual audits, already purchased and filed, will not constitute a legal defence. Continuous, timestamped supplier data will. Harvics provides exactly that — embedded in daily procurement flows, not assembled retrospectively.

🔒

Your Legal Firewall — On the Ground in Pakistan.

Harvics maintains operational presence and data infrastructure at source — not in a European office reviewing third-party reports. Pre-emptive CSRD risk neutralization means we detect supplier violations before they become your liability. Every data point is timestamped, auditable and investor-grade. When regulators arrive, LPP's documentation is already complete. That is not compliance. That is immunity.

📡

Real-Time Supplier Monitoring — Not Quarterly Checkbox Reviews.

The gap between an annual social compliance audit and the next one is where reputational and legal exposure lives. Harvics closes that gap permanently. Supplier-level ESG metrics flow continuously — labour hours, subcontracting deviations, environmental discharge flags — into a single dashboard visible to LPP leadership before the violation becomes a headline.

📈

Investor-Grade Sustainability Record That Moves Your Stock.

Institutional investors are applying ESG screens with increasing precision. A credible, continuous, third-party verified sustainability record is a valuation instrument — not a marketing document. Competitors assembling year-end reports will not compete on this dimension. LPP, with Harvics, will have the only real-time, investor-presentable sustainability data set in its competitive peer group.
